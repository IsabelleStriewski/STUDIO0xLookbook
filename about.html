The Project.

A Digital Lookbook

01. The Vibe.

STUDIO 0x is about bringing the feeling of a fashion magazine to the screen. It’s a collection of visual ideas where I mix clean layouts with bold imagery.

02. The Style.

I focus on high-end typography and lots of empty space. The goal is to let the photos breathe and keep things simple, intentional, and stylish.

03. The Goal.

This project shows how I work with layouts and digital space. It’s a space where fashion meets design in a way that feels modern and curated.
