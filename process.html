The Process.

From Concept to Code

01. Layout & Whitespace. Everything starts with a clean 12-column grid. But instead of filling every box, I focused on "Active Whitespace". By leaving empty areas around the images, the design feels breathable and expensive—just like a high-end fashion magazine.

02. Typography Contrast. The design lives from the clash of two styles. I paired Playfair Display (an elegant Serif font) with Fira Code (a technical Monospace font). This clearly separates the emotional content from the technical details, creating a modern hierarchy.

03. Asymmetry & Flow. Standard websites are often too symmetrical. To create tension, I used "Controlled Asymmetry". By shifting images vertically and ignoring standard grid lines, the eye is guided dynamically across the page. It breaks the monotony.

04. Visual Depth. To make the flat screen feel more tactile, I used layering. The text containers feature a "Frosted Glass" effect (using backdrop-filter), floating above the images. This adds depth and modern elegance to the interface.
